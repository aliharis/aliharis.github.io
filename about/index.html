Hello there.

Feb 2, 2020 19:58 · 129 words · 1 minute read

Hello there, I’m Haris.

I am a product manager and engineer from Maldives. I started coding at the age of 12 by reverse engineering the webpages I would save from the web, and running Python scripts on Symbian OS (PyS60). These days I work mostly with Go and JavaScript.

I am currently working at Payer - A FinTech startup that I co-founded to create a mobile wallet for Maldives. Besides engineering, I am passionate about product management.

Previously I co-founded Lavafoshi, a local music streaming app for Maldives, SparkHub, a consultancy firm working towards straightening the startup ecosystem of the Maldives. I’m also the local ambassador for AngelHack and run the local chapter for Startup Grind.

You can reach out to me on Twitter, or email If you prefer.
