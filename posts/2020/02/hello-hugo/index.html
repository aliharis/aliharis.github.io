Hello Hugo

I started blogging in late 2007 on WordPress, and since then I’ve had multiple blogs (Blogger, WordPress, etc.), every time lost my content for some reason.

It’s been a while since I’ve been thinking about moving to a static site generator and hosting it on Github, so that I don’t ever have to worry about hosting it or losing any content.

I’ve finally done this, moved to Hugo, an excellent static site generator written in Go, which I happen to love.

By the time of writing, I’ve not migrated content from my old blog, I plan to do this over the coming months depending on how much I procrastinate on real works, that’s when I actually get time to do this.
